## Overview

The project involved the design and construction of a modern infinity pool overlooking the sea. The primary focus was on a minimalist aesthetic, durability, and ease of maintenance.

## Execution

The pool was built using high-quality materials and a modern filtration system. Energy-efficient solutions for water heating and circulation were integrated into the design.

## Result

The final result is a premium facility that enhances the guest experience and increases the overall value of the property.
