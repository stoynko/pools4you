An aquapark is more than the sum of its slides and pools — it is a complete experience that creates memories, retains guests and turns a holiday property into a destination that families return to year after year. Designing and building an aquapark demands specific know-how that we at Pools4You have developed with every project we have delivered.

## Attractions and Zones

**Speed Slides** — Adrenaline guaranteed. Tube, open-flume and combination slides with vertical drops, bends and accelerators. We design them in compliance with international safety standards (EN 1069) and dimension throughput capacity against expected visitor flow.

**Family and Multi-Lane Slides** — Built for shared experiences. Wide multi-lane slides allow parents and children to descend simultaneously side by side — a crowd-pleaser for every target audience.

**Lazy River** — A gentle water channel with a soft current along which guests drift on inflatable rings. An essential feature of any family-oriented aquapark, providing continuous movement and extended dwell time in the park.

**Wave Pools** — Artificial waves with adjustable height and frequency that replicate ocean surf. Technologically complex facilities where precise hydraulic calculation is critical — one of our core strengths.

**Children's Aqua Zones** — Safe, brightly coloured and packed with mini-slides, water cannons, spray fountains and interactive water features. Designed with zero water depth and continuous parental supervision in mind.

**Outdoor Jacuzzis and Relaxation Zones** — The quiet harbour amid the park's excitement. Hydrotherapy pools for adults, sun-bathing terraces and swim-up bars generate additional revenue and raise the overall quality of the offer.

**Splash Pads** — Surfaces with integrated water jets, fountains and spray elements with no open water body. Ideal for young children and for areas with limited space.

## Engineering and Technology

An aquapark is an exceptionally engineering-intensive facility. Hydraulic flow modelling, pump station capacity, recirculation and disinfection systems, the structural integrity of towers and platforms — all of this requires multi-disciplinary coordination. At Pools4You we have an in-house team covering all these disciplines and we partner with proven European manufacturers of attraction equipment.

## Theming and Branding

The visual identity of the aquapark is a key marketing tool. We work with thematic design — from colour schemes and mascots to sculptural elements, themed architecture and wayfinding systems. We help our clients build a park with a recognisable character, not just infrastructure.

## Planning and Permits

Building an aquapark requires thorough pre-design analysis: site selection, accessibility, parking, utility connections and environmental impact assessment. We guide our clients through the entire permitting process — from initial consultation with the municipality to obtaining the operating certificate.
