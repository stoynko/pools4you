Spa facilities are not a luxury — they are an investment in the wellbeing of the people who use them and in the value of the property they belong to. At Pools4You we design and build spa zones that combine therapeutic effectiveness with refined aesthetics — spaces where body and mind find genuine balance.

## What a Spa Complex Includes

**Hydrotherapy Pools and Hot Tubs** — Strategically positioned air and water jets stimulate circulation, relieve muscular tension and reduce stress. We offer built-in and freestanding models to suit any interior concept.

**Floatation Pools** — Shallow tanks supersaturated with magnesium sulphate in which the body floats effortlessly. Floatation is a well-established method for deep relaxation, pain relief and improved sleep quality.

**Plunge Pools and Ice Baths** — Contrast temperature therapy is a cornerstone of the Nordic spa tradition. A cold plunge or ice fountain immediately after a hot pool or sauna activates the immune system and accelerates muscular recovery.

**Steam Rooms (Hammam and Turkish Bath)** — Moist heat opens the pores, deep-cleanses the skin and soothes the respiratory tract. We design steam rooms in traditional oriental style or a contemporary minimalist aesthetic.

**Salt Rooms (Halotherapy)** — Walls and air saturated with fine sea salt whose aerosol particles have a documented therapeutic effect on respiratory and skin conditions. Especially suitable for hotel and wellness resort properties.

**Rasul** — An ancient Arabic ritual combining steam, healing mud and aromas. Rasul chambers are among the most exotic additions to a modern spa and attract a discerning clientele.

## Spa Space Design

A successful spa demands careful planning of the flow between wet and dry zones, optimal ventilation, acoustic insulation and the right selection of materials resistant to moisture and temperature fluctuation. Our team of engineers and architects works holistically — from functional zoning to the choice of wall tiles and aromatherapy systems.

## Who It Is For

We design spa zones for hotels and resort complexes, medical and balneological centres, fitness clubs and wellness studios, premium residential developments and private residences. Scale can range from a compact spa bath in an apartment to a multi-hall wellness complex with dozens of treatment zones — we have a solution for every scenario.
